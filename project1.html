WEATHER APP

I developed a weather app using Node.js, Express, and EJS, integrating the OpenWeather API to fetch real-time weather data. Users can enter a city name to get current weather conditions, including temperature, humidity, and a brief description. The app features a responsive design, ensuring a seamless experience across different devices.
